Eastern EU Qualifiers Winners: BetBoom

7 September, Wednesday
In a huge upset BetBoom beat favourites Outsiders and swept then in a 3:0. Outsiders were unbeaten coming into the finals and had not dropped a single game. BetBoom looked very dominiant and strong in the best of five while Outsiders looked nothing like themselves with little coordination and constant misplays.
